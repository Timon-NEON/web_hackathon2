Step 2: Configure Security Settings

Before installing the app from APK file, you need to allow installation of apps from unknown sources:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to "Security" or "Privacy" section
  3. Find the "Unknown sources" or "Install from unknown sources" option and enable it
  4. Confirm the permission if a warning appears

Step 3: Install the App

After downloading the APK file:

  2. Go to "Downloads" folder
  3. Find and tap on ALERTOS.apk file
  4. Tap "Install" and wait for installation to complete
  5. After installation, tap "Open" or find ALERTOS icon on home screen
