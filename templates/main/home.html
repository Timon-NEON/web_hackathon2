Instant Connection with Security

ALERTOS is a system that acts as an intermediary between Support Units and security subjects. The service focuses on instant connection of victims or witnesses with security personnel.

About ALERTOS

User Capabilities

Users can quickly call security through the app and become part of a new security system that makes possible:

  • Call for help with one click
  • Get help from the nearest Support Unit
  • Know the estimated arrival time of Support Unit

Security Organization Capabilities

Support Units have the opportunity to provide their own services in the ALERTOS system, expecting:

  • Acquiring new clients
  • Quick integration with our system through the app
  • Protection requests closest to Support Unit

About the Team

Our team consists of enthusiasts in the field of security and IT, who aim to increase the level of security for conscious people and show that everyone can be under protection.

How It Works

Step 1: Download the App

Download our app from our website.

Step 2: Register

Enter your current data for registration or log into the system. Also provide access to geolocation for the app.

For Users

When you need help, press the "Alert" button. After that, the nearest Support Unit will arrive to you within the specified time to provide services.

For Support Units

When you have the ability to provide protection services, go online. When your Support Unit is selected to provide protection, you will receive a notification.
